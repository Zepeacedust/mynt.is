Members of INA can put objects up for auction at the association's monthly auctions. A variety of materials are offered at each auction. Items can be in a range of conditions, fromheavily used to completely unused.
If INA members want to sell items at the association's auctions, they are advised to contact the auction committee:
  • Alfreð S. Erlingsson, sími: 695-2866
  • Freyr Jóhannesson, sími: 864-1529
  • Helgi E. Jónsson, sími: 821-9696
You can also send an email to myntsafnarafelagid@gmail.comAll emails are answered.
Those who want to convey material to the Mynt-journal are advised to send an email to: myntsafnarafelagid@gmail.com
